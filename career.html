Why Robomiracle

India's leading robot company — why work with us?

Robomiracle Technologies is a DPIIT-recognised robotics startup based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, supported by Startup India and StartupSingam. We design, manufacture, sell, rent, and customise intelligent robots — including humanoid robots (Nova, Unitree G1), quadruped robots (Unitree Go2 Pro), and service robots (Nila, Recepto, Dinebot) — for clients across South India and pan-India. When you join Robomiracle, your work directly powers AI-driven solutions in hospitals, hotels, schools, factories, and events.

Cutting-Edge Robotics Tech

Work hands-on with humanoid robots, quadruped AI systems, ROS, computer vision, and real-time embedded systems — the same stack that powers robots in hospitals and factories across India.

Pan-India Impact

Grow Fast in Robotics

Diverse, Collaborative Team

Mechanical engineers, AI researchers, software developers, and business leaders all under one roof in Coimbatore. Tamil Nadu's most exciting AI and robotics startup team.

Startup India Benefits

Competitive salary, performance incentives, health coverage, flexible work culture, and the pride of working at a DPIIT-recognised Indian robotics company backed by Startup India and StartupSingam.

Ship Real Robots, Fast

FAQs

Frequently asked career questions

Everything you need to know about working at Robomiracle Technologies, India's leading robotics and AI company in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu.

What jobs are available at Robomiracle Technologies?
Robomiracle Technologies is currently hiring for Business Development Manager, Business Development Executive, Junior Robotics Engineer, and Robotics & AI Intern at its Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu headquarters. All roles are full-time or internship-based, with pan-India client exposure.
What is Robomiracle Technologies and what robots do you make?
Robomiracle Technologies is a DPIIT-recognised Indian robotics startup based in Coimbatore. We design, manufacture, sell, rent, and customise intelligent robots — including humanoid robots (Nova, Unitree G1), quadruped robots (Unitree Go2 Pro), service robots (Nila, Recepto), delivery robots (Dinebot), and AI companions (Aurra, LOONA, EMO) — for healthcare, hospitality, education, manufacturing, and events industries across India.
Does Robomiracle offer robotics internships in Coimbatore?
Yes. Robomiracle Technologies offers Robotics & AI internships at its Coimbatore office. Interns work on real humanoid and service robot projects alongside experienced engineers. B.E./B.Tech students in Mechanical Engineering, Electronics, Computer Science, and AI/ML are encouraged to apply.
How do I apply for a job at Robomiracle?
Fill out the application form on this page with your name, email, phone number, the position you're applying for, and your resume as a PDF (max 5MB). The Robomiracle talent team personally reviews every application and responds within 3 business days. No ghosting — guaranteed.
Is Robomiracle a recognised startup under Startup India?
Yes. Robomiracle Technologies is a DPIIT-recognised startup under the Government of India's Startup India programme and is supported by Tamil Nadu's StartupSingam initiative. This makes Robomiracle one of the most credible and fastest-growing robot companies in South India.
What is Robot-as-a-Service (RaaS) and does Robomiracle offer it?
Robot-as-a-Service (RaaS) is a subscription or rental model where businesses pay per use or per month for robot deployment instead of buying outright. Yes — Robomiracle offers RaaS packages for service robots, delivery robots, and greeter robots for hotels, hospitals, corporate offices, and events across India.
