What We Do

Robomiracle is the perfect platform for students in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, and across India to learn robotics, AI, and coding through hands-on innovation.

Robomiracle Technologies — headquartered in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu — is a pioneering robotics education company dedicated to making robotics, artificial intelligence, embedded systems, and coding accessible to school students across India.
