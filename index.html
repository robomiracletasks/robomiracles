Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Robomiracle's robot rental, custom robots, and RaaS services in India.

What is robot rental?

Robot rental allows businesses to use advanced robots for events, exhibitions, and automation without purchasing them outright. Robomiracle offers flexible robot rental packages across India — covering Chennai, Coimbatore, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and more — with full technical support, setup, and operation included.

Where does Robomiracle provide robotics services?

Robomiracle provides robot rental, custom robot development, and Robot as a Service (RaaS) in Chennai, Coimbatore, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Delhi, and across India. We also serve international clients worldwide for large-scale robotics deployments.

What industries use Robomiracle's robots?

Robomiracle serves retail (AI product guidance robots), healthcare (hospital automation robots), hospitality (hotel and restaurant food delivery robots), education (school and college STEM robots), transit (airport and mall guide robots), and events (exhibition and brand promotion robots). Each robot is tailored for its specific industry application in India.

What is Robot as a Service (RaaS)?

Robot as a Service (RaaS) is a subscription-based model that lets businesses access advanced robots without large upfront capital investment. Robomiracle's RaaS model includes deployment, maintenance, software updates, and on-site support — making enterprise-grade robotics accessible for businesses of any size across India.

How do I rent a robot for an event in Chennai or Coimbatore?

To rent a robot for your event, contact Robomiracle at +91-6380473177 or sales@robomiracle.com. We offer robot rentals for weddings, exhibitions, corporate events, mall activations, and brand promotions in Chennai, Coimbatore, and across India with competitive pricing and flexible durations.

What robot models does Robomiracle offer?

Robomiracle offers 8+ robot models: Nila (AI retail robot), Aurra (guide & reception robot), Nexus-A1 (medical robot), Dine Bot (hospitality robot), Nova-A1 (custom service robot), Robert (event robot), Go2 (quadruped robot), and the Unitree G1 (humanoid robot). All models are available for purchase, RaaS subscription, or event rental across India.

Can Robomiracle build a custom robot for my business?

Yes. Robomiracle specializes in custom robot development tailored to your specific business requirements. From concept design and prototyping to manufacturing, deployment, and after-sales support — our engineering team in Coimbatore delivers fully custom robotic solutions for any industry in India.
