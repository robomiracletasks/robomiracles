Intelligent Capabilities

Everything Nila can do for your business — hospital, hotel, school, mall, or event

Mobile App Control (Wi-Fi AP)

Control all robot motions directly from a smartphone. Create custom paths, demo routines, or poses and trigger them instantly during events. Multiple staff members can take over control easily.

Remote Control (~1000 m Range)

6-channel dedicated controller gives precise, real-time control of movement and rotation. Operate off-stage or behind the crowd. Ideal for large venues like malls, expos, and auditoriums across India.

Obstacle Detection (~30 cm)

Front sensors detect nearby objects and people to reduce collision risk. Automatically slows or stops when something is too close. Safer operation in crowded spaces, especially around children.

Multilingual Interaction

AI-Based Greeting & Recognition

Digital Forms & Admin Panel

Visitors enter details directly on the robot's screen. All submissions go to a centralized admin panel for lead management and follow-up. Eliminates manual paperwork with clean data collection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Nila Robot — pricing, features, availability across India

What is the Nila Robot?

Nila is an AI-powered humanoid reception robot by Robomiracle Technologies, Coimbatore. It stands approximately 4.5 ft tall and is designed to greet visitors, provide multilingual assistance in English, Hindi, Tamil and Malayalam, collect visitor data via a 10-inch touchscreen, and navigate autonomously in hotels, hospitals, schools, malls, and corporate offices across India.

How much does the Nila Robot cost in India?

The price of the Nila Robot varies based on configuration and deployment requirements. Submit an inquiry on this page or contact the Robomiracle sales team at sales@robomiracle.com or +91 63804 73177 for a customised quote. Rental options are also available for events and short-term use.

What languages does the Nila Robot speak?

Nila supports multilingual interaction including English, Hindi, Tamil, Malayalam, and other regional Indian languages. You can switch languages easily to match the audience or event location, making it ideal for businesses operating across multiple Indian states.

Can the Nila Robot be used in hospitals and hotels?

Yes. Nila is designed for diverse environments including hospitals, hotels, corporate offices, schools, shopping malls, exhibitions, airports, and banks. It handles reception duties, visitor registration, multilingual assistance, and can display information on its 10-inch touchscreen — reducing workload for front-desk staff.

Is the Nila Robot available for rent in India?

Yes. Robomiracle Technologies offers Nila Robot on a rental basis for events, exhibitions, brand activations, product launches, and short-term deployments across Tamil Nadu and pan-India. Contact the team at +91 63804 73177 or sales@robomiracle.com to check availability and pricing.

How long does the Nila Robot battery last?

Nila runs on a 24V, 25Ah lithium-ion battery providing 4 to 6 hours of continuous operation per charge. The battery charges fully in approximately 2 hours using the included 24V, 5A charger, making it practical for full-day deployments.

Does the Nila Robot avoid obstacles automatically?

Yes. Nila has front-facing obstacle detection sensors that detect objects and people within approximately 30 cm. The robot automatically slows or stops to avoid collisions, making it safe to operate in crowded public spaces, including around children.

Where is Robomiracle Technologies located?

Robomiracle Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is headquartered at Tulasi Chambers, R.S. Puram, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu 641002, India. The company is a Startup India certified robotics company trusted by TCS, SBI, and TVS, serving clients across Tamil Nadu and pan-India.

Can the Nila Robot collect visitor data and leads?

Yes. Nila features a 10-inch touchscreen where visitors fill in digital registration forms. All submissions are automatically sent to a centralised admin panel for lead management and follow-up, eliminating manual paperwork and improving data accuracy.

What is the remote control range of the Nila Robot?

Nila can be operated via a dedicated 6-channel remote controller with a range of approximately 1,000 metres, suitable for large venues like malls, expos, auditoriums, and outdoor events. It also supports mobile app control via Wi-Fi for smartphone-based management.
