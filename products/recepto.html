Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Reception Robot

What is the Reception Robot and how does it work?

The Reception Robot is an AI-powered autonomous service robot designed for reception areas in hotels, hospitals, malls, and offices. It uses advanced computer vision for face recognition, natural language processing for voice interaction, and SLAM technology for autonomous navigation. It can greet visitors, provide information, guide them to destinations, and even perform basic surveillance tasks.

Can I buy or rent the Reception Robot in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu?

Yes, Robomiracle offers both purchase and rental options for the Reception Robot in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, and across India. Our headquarters is in Coimbatore, and we provide installation, training, and support services throughout Tamil Nadu and other major Indian cities.

What industries can benefit from the Reception Robot?

The Reception Robot is versatile and benefits multiple industries including hotels (for guest check-in and concierge services), hospitals (for patient guidance and information), shopping malls (for visitor assistance and directions), corporate offices (for visitor management), educational institutions (for campus guidance), and retail stores (for customer assistance).

Does the Reception Robot require special infrastructure?

No special infrastructure is required. The Reception Robot uses SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) technology to navigate autonomously in any environment. It only needs a standard power outlet for charging and Wi-Fi connectivity for cloud services. Our team handles the initial mapping and setup process.

How long does the battery last and does it charge automatically?

The Reception Robot operates for 8-10 hours on a single charge depending on usage. When the battery level drops below 20%, it automatically returns to its charging dock for recharging. The self-charging capability ensures continuous operation without manual intervention.

Can the Reception Robot be customized for our specific needs?

Yes, the Reception Robot is fully customizable. We can tailor the voice responses, display interface, navigation maps, and even the exterior design to match your brand identity. Our team works with clients to understand specific requirements and implement custom features as needed.
