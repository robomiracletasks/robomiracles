Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about buying, renting, and deploying Nova A1 in India.

Can I buy or rent the Nova A1 robot in Coimbatore?
Yes, Nova A1 is available for both purchase and rent across Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, and Pan-India. We offer flexible rental plans for hospitals, hotels, and schools, and direct sales for educational institutions.
What are the key technical specifications of Nova A1?
Nova A1 features 5 Degrees of Freedom (DOF) for articulation, an 8.7-inch touch display, 3-4 hours of active battery life, and supports multiple programming modes including Teach & Train, Block-based coding, and Python.
Is Nova A1 suitable for school educational programs?
Absolutely. Nova A1 is designed as an Edu Humanoid Agent specifically for STEM education. Its block coding interface is perfect for beginners, while Python support challenges advanced engineering students, making it ideal for Indian curriculums.
Does Robomiracle provide service support across India?
Robomiracle provides comprehensive installation and maintenance support Pan-India. With trusted partners like TCS and SBI, we ensure reliable service delivery and technical support for all our humanoid robots.
