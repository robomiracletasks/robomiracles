Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying and using the Unitree Go2 in India

Where can I buy the Unitree Go2 robot dog in India?

You can buy the Unitree Go2 through Robomiracle Technologies Pvt. Ltd., the authorised distributor based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Robomiracle ships the Go2 pan-India and accepts pre-orders online. Contact the sales team at sales@robomiracle.com or call +91 63804 73177.

What is the difference between Unitree Go2 AIR, PRO, and EDU?

The Go2 AIR is the entry-level model with 2.5 m/s speed and WiFi-only connectivity. The Go2 PRO adds 4G eSIM, voice commands, ISS 2.0 intelligent side-follow, and a faster 3.7 m/s speed — ideal for enterprise and security use. The Go2 EDU is the research variant with 5 m/s top speed, 13 kg payload capacity, NVIDIA Orin NX/Nano support, foot force sensors, and a full secondary development SDK for ROS integration.

Can I rent a Unitree Go2 robot dog in India?

Yes. Robomiracle Technologies offers rental options for the Unitree Go2 series for events, exhibitions, research pilots, and short-term deployments across India. Rental enquiries can be sent to sales@robomiracle.com with your event location, duration, and intended use.

What is 4D LiDAR L1 on the Unitree Go2?

The 4D LiDAR L1 is a sensor that detects and maps the 3D environment in real time, including moving objects (the 4th dimension being time or motion). It enables the Go2 to perform precise obstacle detection and agile avoidance across all terrain types — indoors, outdoors, and on rough or uneven surfaces.

What is ISS 2.0 side-follow on the Unitree Go2 Pro?

ISS 2.0 (Intelligent Side-follow System 2.0) allows the Go2 Pro to autonomously follow a person or object from the side at a set distance, adjusting speed and direction in real time using wireless vector positioning and LiDAR data. It is ideal for security patrols, logistics, and content creation applications.

Does the Unitree Go2 work on rough terrain and stairs?

Yes. The Unitree Go2 is designed for all terrains. It can climb steps up to 15 cm in height and handle slopes up to 40° (PRO and EDU variants). The 4D LiDAR enables real-time terrain mapping and dynamic gait adjustment, making it suitable for stairs, gravel, grass, and uneven floors.

Can the Unitree Go2 EDU be programmed with custom code?

Yes. The Go2 EDU ships with a secondary development SDK that supports ROS-based robotics research and custom application development. It also accepts an external NVIDIA Orin NX or Nano AI compute module, making it a powerful platform for AI, computer vision, and autonomous systems research in India.

What industries and applications use the Unitree Go2 in India?

The Unitree Go2 is deployed across research and education, security and surveillance, industrial inspection, entertainment and events, hospitality, and smart infrastructure monitoring. Robomiracle has delivered robots to enterprise clients including TCS, SBI, and TVS across India.
