Where can I buy a service robot in India?
You can buy a wide range of service robots directly from Robomiracle. We are based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, but we ship pan-India. Our inventory includes the DanceBot Robert, hospitality units like Nila and Aurra, and industrial quadrupeds like Unitree Go2.
Do you offer robot rentals for hospitals and hotels?
Yes, Robomiracle offers flexible rental options for businesses. We provide service robots for hospitals, hotels, and events across India. Our rental fleet includes reception bots, room service units, and disinfection robots to suit your specific operational needs.
