Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Dine Robot — India's autonomous food serving and delivery robot for restaurants, hotels, hospitals, and cafes.

The Dine Robot by ROBOMIRACLE is an autonomous food serving and delivery robot for restaurants, hotels, hospitals, and cafes across India. It autonomously navigates through spaces, carries up to 30 kg of food across three trays, interacts with guests via a 12-inch touchscreen and 20W speakers, and operates continuously for 12–24 hours on a single charge — reducing labour costs and improving service speed.

The Dine Robot carries up to 30 kg total — approximately 10 kg per tray across its three serving trays. Each tray is approximately 41–43 cm wide, making it suitable for full restaurant-sized plates, bowls, and beverages simultaneously.

The Dine Robot runs on a 24V, 25Ah lithium-ion battery with an estimated backup of 12–24 hours. It fully recharges in just 2–3 hours using the 24V, 5A charger — ideal for full restaurant service shifts without interruption.
