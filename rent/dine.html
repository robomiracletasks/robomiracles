What Makes Dinebot India's Best Restaurant Robot

Advanced AI capabilities built for Indian hospitality environments

Autonomous LIDAR Navigation

Real-time environmental mapping using cutting-edge LIDAR sensors. Navigates crowded dining halls, banquet spaces, and event venues without human intervention — safe and precise every time.

Multi-Tray Food Delivery

Carry multiple trays with a payload capacity up to 30 kg. Delivers food and beverages to multiple tables in a single trip — dramatically improving service efficiency and reducing labour costs.

Real-Time Object Detection

AI-powered computer vision detects and avoids obstacles in real time — guests, children, furniture, and moving objects. Safe, smooth operation in dynamic restaurant environments across India.

Fully Customizable Design

Custom colors, logos, display screens, and welcome messages in Tamil, Hindi, and English. Every Dinebot becomes a branded ambassador tailored to your restaurant or hotel identity.

Interactive Touch Display

An integrated touchscreen lets guests view menus, place orders, or receive personalized greetings — creating memorable dining experiences that set your establishment apart.

Long Battery + Auto-Dock

Designed for full-shift operation. The Dinebot automatically returns to its charging dock when battery runs low, ensuring uninterrupted service through busy lunch and dinner rushes.
