Controls

Simple Controls, Maximum Fun

Every button on Robert is designed to be intuitive — even for young children

Pause / Play Button
Press to pause or resume your music playback at any time during the performance.
LED Light
Built-in LED lighting that activates during dance mode, adding a visual spectacle to every performance.
Left Button
Short press: previous song. Long press: volume down.
Right Button
Short press: next song. Long press: volume up.
Power / Dance Switch
Long press 5 seconds to power on or off. Short press to stop or start dancing.
TF Card Slot & Charging Interface
Insert a TF card to play music directly without Bluetooth. Charge via the dedicated charging interface at the base.
Space Robert App

Full Control in Your Hands

Writing / Programming Mode
Write your own custom action sequences — set rhythm, lighting, and movements, then save and play them back. An ideal entry point for kids to learn robotics and programming logic.
Music & Rhythm Control
Add your own music tracks via the app and sync Robert's dance movements to the rhythm. Create fully personalized performances for any song in your collection.
Lighting & Speed Settings
Pick from multiple LED lighting colors and adjust Robert's movement speed — from a slow groove to a fast-paced performance — all from the app interface.

Robert DanceBot is Robomiracle's fun dancing robot speaker designed for kids, students, and families. It combines interactive dancing movements with high-quality audio (3 Ohm / 5W speaker), Bluetooth connectivity, a TF card slot, LED lights, and app-enabled control — making it both a great entertainment gadget and an entry-level robotics companion for schools and hobby clubs.

Robert DanceBot specifications: Dimensions 20 × 12 × 9 cm. Speaker: 3 Ohm / 5W for crisp, clear audio. Battery: 3.7V / 1800mAh rechargeable. Connectivity: Bluetooth wireless pairing. Storage: TF card slot for offline music playback. Lighting: built-in LED with multiple color options. Controls: physical buttons and the Space Robert companion app.

Robert DanceBot has intuitive physical controls: Left button — short press for previous song, long press for volume down. Right button — short press for next song, long press for volume up. The Space Robert companion app offers two main modes. Writing/Programming mode: create custom action sequences by setting rhythm, lighting, and movements, then save and play them back. You can also add your own music tracks and sync Robert's dance to them.
