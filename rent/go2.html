What Makes Subramani India's Best AI Dog Robot

AI-Powered Navigation

Advanced autonomous navigation with real-time environmental awareness. Subramani maps its surroundings and moves intelligently through crowded spaces, stairs, uneven terrain, and dynamic environments without human intervention.

Voice Interaction & Smart Response

Equipped with a sophisticated voice interaction system, Subramani responds to commands, greets visitors, and engages audiences — in English, Tamil, and Hindi — making it perfect for events, exhibitions, and customer-facing deployments.

Real-Time Obstacle Detection

AI-powered computer vision instantly detects and avoids people, furniture, and moving objects. Safe, smooth operation across indoor and outdoor environments — hospitals, malls, corporate campuses, and public spaces across India.

Fully Customizable Design

Custom colors, brand logos, LED panels, and display screens transform Subramani into a branded ambassador. Whether for a product launch, corporate event, or security deployment, every Subramani is tailored to your identity.

Remote Control & Automation

Real-time remote control allows operators to take command when needed, while autonomous mode handles routine patrols, greetings, and guided tours. Flexible control modes adapt to any deployment requirement.

Long Battery & Efficient Operation

Battery-efficient design ensures long operational hours ideal for full-day events, overnight security patrols, and multi-shift deployments. Automatic low-battery return keeps operations uninterrupted across demanding schedules.
