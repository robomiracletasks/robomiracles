What Makes Nila India's Best Reception Robot

Advanced AI capabilities built for Indian hospitality, events, and corporate environments

Mobile App Control (Wi-Fi AP)

Control all robot motions directly from a smartphone — no cables needed. Create custom paths, demo routines, and poses triggered instantly during events. Multiple staff can operate simultaneously.

6-Channel Remote (up to ~1000 m)

Dedicated long-range controller for precise real-time control of movement and rotation. The operator stays off-stage or behind the crowd while Nila performs — ideal for malls, expos, and auditoriums.

Obstacle Detection (~30 cm)

Front sensors detect nearby objects and people to reduce collision risk. Automatically slows or stops when something is too close — safer operation in crowded spaces, especially around children and families.

Multilingual AI Interaction

Conversations in English, Hindi, Tamil, Malayalam, and other regional languages. Switch languages easily to match the audience or event location — making every guest feel comfortable and welcome.

AI Greeting with Image Processing

Camera detects approaching visitors and triggers automatic welcome messages. Creates a powerful "wow" factor at receptions, exhibitions, and brand activations — fully hands-free and automatic.

Photo Booth with 0–180° Rotation

Camera-based control with smooth head/body rotation from 0 to 180 degrees enables natural turns toward guests. Perfect for interactive photo sessions, stage presentations, and demo routines at weddings and events.

Digital Visitor Registration

Guests enter details directly on Nila's 10-inch touchscreen. All submissions sync to a centralized admin panel for lead management and follow-up — eliminating manual paperwork at expos and corporate events.

10-Inch Display & Windows OS

Full-featured touchscreen with Windows OS for complete compatibility. Backend platform for ads, customization, and media. Run presentations, menus, custom brand content, or interactive forms seamlessly.

4–6 Hour Battery Life

24V 25Ah lithium-ion battery delivers 4–6 hours of continuous operation per charge. Full recharge in approximately 2 hours — designed for all-day events, weddings, and multi-session corporate functions.
