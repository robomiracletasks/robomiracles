What Makes Kevin India's Most Advanced Humanoid Robot

Cutting-edge AI and robotics capabilities built for Indian enterprise and event environments

Full-Body Articulated Motion

Kevin features 43 degrees of freedom across arms, legs, torso, and hands — enabling human-like walking, gesturing, waving, and object manipulation. The most expressive humanoid robot available for rent in India.

Real-Time AI Conversation

Powered by advanced language models, Kevin converses naturally in English, Tamil, and Hindi. It answers questions, delivers scripted presentations, and responds to live audience inputs — making every interaction memorable.

Autonomous Navigation

Kevin maps and navigates complex environments autonomously using LIDAR and depth sensors. It moves through event halls, exhibition floors, and research labs without human guidance — safely and precisely.

Real-Time Object Detection

AI-powered computer vision enables Kevin to detect people, obstacles, and objects in real time. It avoids collisions and adapts to dynamic environments — essential for safe operation in crowded event spaces across India.

Interactive Chest Display

An integrated touchscreen display on Kevin's chest allows guests to interact directly — view content, receive information, or trigger custom presentations. A completely new dimension of audience engagement for Indian events.

Enterprise-Grade Customization

Kevin can be programmed for custom scripted interactions, branded presentations, multilingual greetings, and specialized research tasks. Fully configurable for any enterprise, institutional, or event requirement across India.
